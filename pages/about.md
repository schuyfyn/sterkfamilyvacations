## About the Collection

This is a collection of family vacation pictures designed for my Information Organization class at the University of Iowa's School of Library and Information Science. It allows users to search through pictures by subject, location, date, and more. This collection was built using Collection Builder-Git Hub. Project components include:

- a CSV of robust descriptive and administrative metadata
- a folder of JPG images
- thoughtful editing of the final site's theme and layout

The repository features 15 images from family vacations to Duluth, MN and Minnesota's Northern Shore, starting in 2006 and going to 2020. These vacations have become core memories for my family; images depict times we shared at different ages, visiting and revisting favorite locations and spending quality time together. I built this collection with my family in mind, hoping they would enjoy looking through the memories we shared (or at least this small fraction of them).
